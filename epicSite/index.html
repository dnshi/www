Project Description:

This project is based on a tutorial, which includes functions of the Unity3D and concepts of designing and developing a video game. The environment is the most successful part over this project, involving shaders, textures, skybox, hills, environmental sound, smoke/fire (Particle Effects), fog, 3D modelling and lights.

This game is cross-platform. It can be built on Windows, Mac and Web. C# is used as scripting language. Listener/Collider functions are widely implemented, for actions or plots are triggered if a player meets conditions such as the door opening automatically when a player collects all batteries and is close to the door.

Game Description:

Main character has awoken on a desert island, with only an active volcano and a mysterious cabin for company. The goal is to survive by lighting a fire to signal for help and keep warm. In order to light the fire the player must acquire the matches that are locked away in the cabin. To unlock the cabin the player must pick up several power cells to fuel the generator that powers the door to the cabin. This would be simple except that the final power cell is locked into a minigame requiring the player to knock down timed targets at a coconut shy shooting gallery—knock them all down simultaneously, and the player will win that all important final cell!

Key words: Unity3D, Game Concepts, Graphics Design and C# Programming
